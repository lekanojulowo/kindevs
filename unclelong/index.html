Steps on how to prepare pie

  1. Seeve the flour into a pan
  2. Mix the butter andsugar
  3. Add the baking powder and the yeast into the flour and mix
  4. Mix the eggs and milk together
  5. Add all the three mixture together and mix till smooth
  6. Put a small quantity on a clean surface, roll and form into any shape, then bake
  7. Prepare with your other ingredients
